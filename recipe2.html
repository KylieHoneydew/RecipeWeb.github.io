Yum Yum Kitchen

Fresh Food Done Right

Ingredients

Serves 4

  • 1 loaf of French bread or Italian bread
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
  • 4-5 cloves garlic, minced (or 1-2 teaspoons garlic powder)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Salt, to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the softened butter, minced garlic (or garlic powder), parsley, and a pinch of salt. You can also add the Parmesan cheese for extra flavor if you like.
  3. Take your loaf of bread and cut it in half lengthwise, or slice it into thick pieces if you prefer smaller servings.
  4. Evenly spread the garlic butter mixture over the cut sides of the bread. Make sure to cover the surface well for that rich garlic flavor.
  5. Place the bread on a baking sheet, buttered side up. Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes, until the edges are crispy and golden.
  6. For extra crispiness, you can turn on the broiler for the last 1-2 minutes of baking, but watch carefully to avoid burning.
  7. Remove the bread from the oven, slice, and serve warm.
